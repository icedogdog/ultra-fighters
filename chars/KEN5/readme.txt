Special Moves
----------------------------------------------

Super Moves 
----------------------------------------------
Shyrureppa
D, DF, F, 2 punches
Shippujinraikyaku
D, DB, B, 2 kicks
Shinryuken
D, DF, F, 2 kicks
Alpha Counter *1/2 bar
D, DF, F, 2 punches or 2 kicks

*Info on alpha counter: Alpha counters are done when you block. If you do the motion
when your blocking, he should do a dragon punch. It's pretty useful, especial agains moves
with lots of block damage.
